WHAT IS CLIMATE CHANGE

Causes

Climate change is caused by activities such as burning fossil fuels, this increase greenhouse gas emissions and causes a warming effect in the atmosphere.

Effects

The effects of climate change include rising temperatures, sea-level rise, more frequent extreme weather events, and the loss of biodiversity, which can have significant impacts on the ecosystem.

Solutions

While there is no single solution to climate change, it is crucial that we take action to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and limit the impacts of climate change.

WHAT CAN YOU DO

Reduce energy usage

Switch off lights and unplug devices when not in use. Invest in energy-efficient appliances.

Be proactive

Write or meet with your local representatives and ask for their support for environmental initiatives.

Eat more green

Reduce meat consumption and eat more locally sourced and organic food. Start a kitchen garden.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

Use reusable bags, cups, and cutlery instead of disposable plastic. Recycle as much as possible.
